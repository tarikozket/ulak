what is "ulak"?

ulak is a simple notification bar plugin for jquery. it's installation and usage is very easy.

1. include jquery to your page.
2. include ulak.js to your page.
3. include ulak.css to your page.
4. execute it!

demos

Click for "error"

ulak({"text":"an error occured","type":"error","timeout":2000});

*>Click for "success"*

ulak({"text":"success","type":"success","timeout":2000});

Click for "warning"

ulak({"text":"what are you doing? be careful...","type":"warning","timeout":2000});

Click for "info"

ulak({"text":"what information you'll give to people?","type":"info","timeout":2000});

modal integrated

I'm still working on it but modal is already integrated. because next version will come out with modal confirm box. it's not just for confirm box, you can use modal with all the types.
